Rules

  1. The first fighter to hit their opponent's edge of the screen wins the round. (Ties are possible.)
  2. Move your weapon up and down to parry your opponent's attacks and pass their guard.
  3. If you hit your opponent's weapon head-on, or if you are parried into the ceiling or floor, you will be stunned.
  4. While stunned, you can move your weapon up and down, but you cannot attack.
